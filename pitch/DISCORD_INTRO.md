## Etiquette reminders

- **Don't post the same message in 3+ channels at once** — gets flagged as spam.
- **Don't tag mods or co-founders directly first time** — wait for organic engagement.
- **Engage in the channel for a day or two** before posting your intro. Answer someone else's question first if possible.
- **Read the pinned rules** in each channel before posting (channels often have a "no self-promo without 7 days of activity" rule).
- **Follow up in DMs only after explicit invitation**. Cold DMs to mods rarely land well.

## Optional follow-ups (after first post lands)

- React to questions other devs ask. Be helpful.
- Share concrete progress updates (not promo): "deployed contracts to Saigon today, here's the addresses", "added X cards this week".
- Offer to playtest other Builders Program submissions in exchange for feedback on yours.
